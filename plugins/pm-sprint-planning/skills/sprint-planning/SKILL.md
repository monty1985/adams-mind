# Sprint Planning

## Phase 1: Preparation

- Review the backlog and identify candidates for this sprint
- Check team capacity (account for time off, meetings, and overhead)
- Identify sprint goals aligned with roadmap priorities
- Ensure top backlog items are refined and ready for planning

## Phase 2: Story Selection

- Walk through candidate stories with the team
- Estimate each story using story points (Fibonacci: 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13)
- Discuss complexity, unknowns, and dependencies before pointing
- Select stories that fit within team velocity and capacity

## Phase 3: Commitment

- Finalize the sprint scope based on capacity and velocity
- Assign story owners and identify any pairing needs
- Document the sprint goal in one clear sentence
- Confirm acceptance criteria for all committed stories
- Record the plan in your project tracker and share with stakeholders
