# Backlog Grooming

## Phase 1: Review

- Pull up the full backlog and scan for stale items (older than 2 sprints with no activity)
- Identify duplicate stories and merge or close them
- Archive items that are no longer relevant or have been superseded
- Flag stories that are blocked on external dependencies

## Phase 2: Refinement

- Break down large epics or stories that exceed 8 story points
- Add or clarify acceptance criteria for any story missing them
- Attach designs, mockups, or API specs where applicable
- Ensure each ready story has a clear definition of done

## Phase 3: Prioritization

- Re-rank the backlog based on current business priorities
- Use a scoring framework (RICE, MoSCoW, or value vs. effort matrix)
- Confirm top 10–15 items are sprint-ready with estimates
- Communicate priority changes to the engineering team before the next sprint
