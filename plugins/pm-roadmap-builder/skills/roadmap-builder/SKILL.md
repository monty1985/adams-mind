# Roadmap Builder

## Phase 1: Goal Setting

- Define the planning horizon (quarter, half-year, annual)
- Align on top-level business and product objectives
- Identify key stakeholders and their priorities
- Gather input from engineering, sales, support, and leadership

## Phase 2: Prioritization

- List all candidate initiatives and features
- Apply RICE scoring (Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort) or MoSCoW (Must/Should/Could/Won't)
- Group items into themes or pillars for clarity
- Resolve conflicts and negotiate trade-offs with stakeholders

## Phase 3: Timeline

- Place prioritized items into time buckets (Now / Next / Later)
- Identify dependencies between initiatives
- Flag risks and assumptions for each major item
- Publish the roadmap and schedule regular review cadences
